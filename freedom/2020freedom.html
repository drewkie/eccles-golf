The 2020 Freedom Open

The Freedom Open singles tournament was played on July 4th this year with a father/son tournament preceding it on the 3rd. Below are all the participants of the tournament.

 

The novel coronaviris pandemic did not stop the greatest tradition in golf. The clubhouse was very accommodating to players and keeping all participants safe. Everyone even got their own chips and dip if they wished.

 

The weather for the tournament was the best that could be hoped for, it being July in Oklahoma after all. Partly cloudy with a light breeze and highs in the lower 90's. Also the course was in top notch condition, the course manager really out did himself this year.

The course sign greeted the participants at the first tee, and set the expectations for the rest of the course.
