The 2024 Freedom Open

The 2024 Freedom Open will be played on Saturday, June 29th. That is the weekend before the 4th of July. We have an exciting set of story lines for you this year. Two competitors playing for the first ever three time winner, one player is charging back stronger than ever from surgery, and of course last years winner, Riley, is looking to be the first back to back winner. And it just so happens to be the 11th Freedom Open, which is Riley's favorite number...

You won't want to miss out! See you all soon!
