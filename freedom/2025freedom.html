The 2025 Freedom Open

The 2025 Freedom Open was played on Saturday, July 5th, and it came down to the wire! Kelley emerged victorious from a heated final round for his first Freedom Open win. Kit was so close to his third championship he could taste it, but Kelley held him off. We were one stroke away from seeing a 3 hole playoff (a new championship rule), but no such luck this year. There is always next year though! Maybe Kelley will be the first back to back champion and join the other two-time champions. You'll have come out and see!

See you all soon!
