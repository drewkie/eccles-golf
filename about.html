Our Championship Course

Designed by the Great Architect, the 9 hole backyard championship course at Eccles Country Club opened by invitation only in 2010 to much adulation. Golf experts agree that this is the best backyard golf course in the country, and provides an amazing and an unforgettable country club experience.

In addition to our well maintained greens, enjoy the stories, the fun, and the beer, but please drink responsibly. Eccles Country Club will make your golf experience one worth remembering.
