The 2025 Freedom Open was held on Saturday, July 5th. We could not have asked for better weather and the course played great! We'll keep you updated for 2026 and hopefully we'll see you there!

 

The winner of 2025 Freedom Open was Kelley. This was, surprisingly, Kelley's first coveted champions white hat. It was neck and neck on the final round with Kit and Kelley, and it came down to the last stroke. Some were hoping for a 3 hole playoff, but no such luck this year.
