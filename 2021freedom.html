The 2021 Freedom Open

The 2021 Freedom Open was be played on Saturday, July 3rd, and the winner was Drew. It was a hard fought battle and we finally have our first 2 time winner. Drew had a miraculous last two holes in the championship round. He was down 7 points to Kit and was able to make that up.

We'll see if Drew can be the first time back to back and 3 time winner next year, or you can come out and make sure that it doesn't happen.
